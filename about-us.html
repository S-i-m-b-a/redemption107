About Us

 

Our passion for wood focused furniture started over a decade ago. Our skilled team uses this experience to source only the very best raw materials to design and manufacture unique, statement furniture pieces for individuals, homes, restaurants, lodges and hotels.

 

We are based in Centurion, South Africa, making us ideally situated to distribute across Gauteng, South Africa and beyond.

 

Our vision is to translate nature`s unique images into functional, eye-catching, investment furniture pieces.
